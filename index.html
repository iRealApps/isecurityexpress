Client Category

One of the largest agri solutions company.

Objective

Engage with the farmer community across the agri-value chain by owning the agri knowledge space.

Solution

  • iRealities conceptualized, designed and developed a robust digital platform with a farmer centric approach to build integrated network in the agri-community aimed at enhancing their knowledge to help them to optimize cost and increase farm yields.
  • With intelligent utilisation of modern information technology, content classification method and content collation process, the platform allows for collaboration and knowledge sharing to address the key pain area.
  • A strong recommendation engine backed by machine learning provided customised solutions based on crops, soil and area.
  • A massive knowledge repository was created, which the farmers could access on the go. The content for knowledge repository was re-curated in collaboration with some of the finest agri experts and converted into multi-lingual multimedia and interactive format with use of animation and visuals to create a engaging experience.
  • To ensure maximum customer touch-points the platform had multiple frontends in the form of web portal, mobile app, kioks as well as integration with call center.

Client Category

One of the largest integrated infrastructure conglomerate of India.

Objective

Gather real-time news of the market and competitors vital for business insights and intelligence.

Solution

Client Category

Educational Institutes

Objective

Revamp of incumbent elearning material as well as textbook content as it was having attention less than 5 minutes which was a strain on the ability of the teacher to keep the student engaged.

Solution

  • By combing the key pedagogical approaches we created a engaging learning course that was a blend of story-telling, interactivity and gamification.
  • The thought was educate with play. Hence we created 3D animated character based stories that resonated with the children and interactive activities like games and tasks.
  • Simplicity, engagement and persuasion were the critical components of education content.
  • The framework was deployed in multiple devices ipad, mobile and classroom and enabled :
    • Presenter Driven - Learning
    • Self – Learning
    • Guided – Learning
  • The end-product of this approach helps organisation and educational institutes who work with us to make learning exciting and fulfilling.
